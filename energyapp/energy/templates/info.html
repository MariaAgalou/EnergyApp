This application was created in order to be a useful tool for the electrical energy providers.
The purpose of this application is to use it in order to take the appropriate measures or actions
and make the right decisions when it comes to electrical energy consumption data management.

By using it, an electrical energy provider can:

1. See an overview with some statistical data about the electrical energy consumption made
by his customers the past 24 hours. Click here

2. See how successful or not the predictions that this application makes are. He can see through
diagrams as well as statistics if the estimated energy consumption is close to the real energy
consumption an dtherefore, realise how accurate these predictions are. Click here

3. Have an estimation about the future electrical energy consumption. He can predict the
consumption about the next day, week or month and see the results with the help of appropriate
diagrams. Click here

4. See a list containing all his customers as well as some statistical data about the energy
consumption that each one of them haw made in the past 30 days. He can also view more details
about a specific customer by just using his unique id. Click here
